# Cryptovid-21
Cryptovid gets its origin from the cryptonite, a crystal rock found on Crypton. 
Along millenars some nano particules from cryptonite crystal becomes higly volalite, this what we call now "Cryptovid".

Cryptovid is generally dangerous for humans but Isotope 19 of the Cryptovid is somewhat different.
Until nowadays medical research never found any bad side effect on humans and any kind of life. 

> Crypton's legend tells the isotope 19 of Cryptovid (Cryptovid-19) brings prosperity and long life to people lucky enough to get some particules of them.
